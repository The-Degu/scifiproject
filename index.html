The Crew

Learn about the crew who are exploring Nótt! The rotating crew of six who man the FREE mission are the pride and joy of Valhalla Space Group. Our diverse group of people moving in and out of our interplanetary xenobiology unit are the best of the best, from cytologist to ecologist and veterinary neurologist FREE is proud of the discoveries and creativity of our crew.

Learn about what it takes to get into the FREE program and be one of our xeno-ecologist . You don’t just need academics to qualify! you might be surprised to find the additional skillset of the people who live on the planet of Nótt. Engineers and mechanics keep our outpost running, while medical doctors and Human Habitation experts make sure our crew stays happy and healthy on their missions. We also have an ever changing rotation of industry experts, from the pharmaceutical to Homeside experts looking into how the life geology, ecology, and pharmacology of these outer planets could help us here at Home to recover and explore our own dwellings

Our Discoveries

The FREE mission has many goals and has made many discoveries from single cells to the charismatic animals shown here. While every discovery is groundbreaking This is just the tip of the iceberg as large mammal-like animals only make up a small portion of any planet’s ecosystem.

From the tall Loricant to the ferocious Faux-Cat, the animals found on Nótt are both not dissimilar to our own and completely unique. So don’t let that fur and those eyes fool you these creatures act, live, and exist in a way completely different then something from Home.
