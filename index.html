the crew of FREE is the pride and joy of Valhalla Space Group. Our diverse group of people moving in and out of our interplanetary xenobiology unit are the best of the best, from cytologist to ecologist and veterinary neurologist FREE is proud of the discoveries and creativity of our constantly changing crew.

In addition to academics you might be suppressed to find the additional skillset of the people who live on the planet of Nótt. Engineers and mechanics keep our outpost running, while medical doctors and Human Habitation experts make sure our crew stays happy and heaty on their missions. We also have an everchanging rotation of industry experts, from the pharmaceutical to Homeside experts looking into how the life geology and overall ecology might help us here at Home to recover and explore our own dwellings
